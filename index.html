Game Settings Manager

Instructions

  1. Log in to Tranquility with the character you want to copy FROM
  2. Log in to Thunderdome and create your character if necessary
  3. Close all game instances
  4. In the From column, select Tranquility and the account and character with the most recent timestamp.
  5. In the To column, select your Thunderdome account and character.
  6. Click Copy
  7. Profit!
